Eddie Morales

White Cord

Eddie is from Sao Paulo, Brazil. He
began to train capoeira when he was
seven. He came to the U.S. as a foreign
exchange student and continued training
here. After much dedication, he earned
the white cord. Soon after he opened
his own studio. He has been now been
instructing capoeira for over 7 years.
He really enjoys teaching capoeira.
