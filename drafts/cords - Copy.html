The Cord System

As with many, if not all, forms of martial arts, a cord system is incorporated into its school system. In capoeira, there is not an official cord system. Instead, it is based upon the individual with the highest cord in its scool. Even when a student with a lower cord branches off, they still follow and respect the system which their mestre has set up. Usually, the cord colors are represented with different shades of the colors found on the Brazilian flag. As to Rexburg Capoeira, its colors and levels follow those of CapuraGinga, its mother school, and consist of the following cords and colors (multiple cords are used on those with multiple colors).
